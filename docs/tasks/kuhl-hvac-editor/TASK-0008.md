## 実装詳細

### 1. DuctSegmentNode・DuctFittingNode 🔵

**信頼性**: 🔵 *interfaces.ts DuctSegmentNode*

DuctSegmentNode(`duct_`): start, end, shape(rectangular|circular), width?, height?, diameter?, material(5種), insulation, medium(4種: supply, return, outside_air, exhaust), startPortId, endPortId, systemId?, calcResult?。DuctFittingNode(`dfit_`): BaseNode拡張。DuctMedium, DuctMaterial, DuctShape, DuctCalcResult 型定義。

**実装ファイル**: `packages/kuhl-core/src/schema/nodes/duct-segment.ts`, `duct-fitting.ts`

### 2. PipeSegmentNode・PipeFittingNode 🔵

**信頼性**: 🔵 *interfaces.ts PipeSegmentNode*

PipeSegmentNode(`pipe_`): start, end, nominalSize, material(8種), medium(6種), insulation, startPortId, endPortId, systemId?, calcResult?。PipeFittingNode(`pfit_`): BaseNode拡張。PipeMedium, PipeMaterial, PipeCalcResult 型定義。

**実装ファイル**: `packages/kuhl-core/src/schema/nodes/pipe-segment.ts`, `pipe-fitting.ts`

### 3. SystemNode・SupportNode・ArchitectureRefNode 🔵

**信頼性**: 🔵 *interfaces.ts より*

SystemNode(`sys_`): systemName, systemType(8種), color?, memberIds。SupportNode(`sup_`): BaseNode拡張。ArchitectureRefNode(`arch_`): ifcFilePath, ifcModelId?, geometryData?, levelMapping?。

**実装ファイル**: `packages/kuhl-core/src/schema/nodes/system.ts`, `support.ts`, `architecture-ref.ts`

### 4. AnyNode ユニオン・types.ts 🔵

**信頼性**: 🔵 *設計文書 §2.3*

z.discriminatedUnion('type', [...]) で全24ノードタイプを統合。AnyNodeType, AnyNodeId 型エクスポート。

**実装ファイル**: `packages/kuhl-core/src/schema/types.ts`
